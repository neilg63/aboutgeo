Reverse Postcode Finder and Geographic Explorer

This applications links an OpenStreetMap covering the whole world with two geographic databases and remote Web services. The first contains historical and current timezone data and over 2 million place names around the world with related while the second over 1 million UK post codes with addresses. Extra information such as weather, nearby places of interests and related Wikipedia articles come from Geonames.org. and are cached for varying lengths of time.

The backend uses two micro-services both available on GitHub at GeoFinder and GeoTime

Astronomical Calculator

This shows the positions, movements and phases of the Ascendant, Sun, Moon and Planet at any time and from any location on Earth. It's powered by my open-source AstroCalcApi, which in turn harnesses all the core feature of the famous SwissEphmeris calculation engine.

Current and Historical TimeZone Finder

This is a simple React app that harnesses the GeoFinder backend to find the correct time zone for any of 44691 towns and cities with some sunrise, sunset and moon phase data provided by AstroCalc.
